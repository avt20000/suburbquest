You snap awake. How long have you been asleep? Where are you? Did you miss your stop? You used to take the bus all the time, but never this far into Redmond. You pull the cord, to make sure you can get out at the next stop and regroup.

You check your phone, which has a worryingly low amount of battery left. There’s a text there from your classmate: “wya”. You’re already at least 45 minutes late, and you’re wondering if it’s even worth it to meet up at this point. The sign by the bus says “Avondale”. Avondale. You’re so far from home it’s not even funny.

Thoughts are swirling around your head as the panic begins to climb. How could you have fallen asleep? How did you miss your stop?! It was so simple and you couldn’t even manage to do it correctly. You can’t do anything right. Everything you try falls apart. Why do you even bother? What’s wrong with you?!?
